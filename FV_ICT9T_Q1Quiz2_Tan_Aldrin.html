This is Jujutsu Kaisen 0, also called JJK.

JJK is an animated movie created by MAPPA in December 24, 2021.

High school student Yuta Okkotsu is bullied and injured by a Cursed Spirit. The Jujutsu Society wishes to have him killed, but Satoru Gojo, a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu Technical High, recruits him to join the school in November 2016. Yuta explains that the Cursed Spirit is Rika, a childhood friend who died in a freak accident and became an overprotective spirit. Yuta meets fellow students Maki Zen'in, Toge Inumaki, and Panda, each with unique abilities.

During his first mission with Maki, Yuta summons Rika on his own for the first time. Three months pass, and he grows close to them. One day, a high-level Curse attacks Toge and Yuta, led by Suguru Geto, a former student and old friend of Gojo. Geto declares war to activate a portal to the underworld, releasing a thousand Curses to remove non-sorcerer humans. Gojo realizes this and sends Inumaki and Panda back to protect Yuta and Maki during the night of Geto's attack. Geto overpowers them, leaving only Yuta conscious. Enraged, Yuta promises himself as a sacrifice to Rika to strengthen their bond.

Gojo confirms that Yuta was the one who inadvertently cursed Rika through unspecified ties to royal bloodlines within Japan's caste system. After breaking the curse, Rika can pass on peacefully after Yuta continues training as a sorcerer within the institution. In a post-credits scene, Geto's former ally Miguel eats lunch with Yuta in Kenya, and Gojo joins them.
